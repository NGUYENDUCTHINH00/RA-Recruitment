        Business Development Manager

        SAVVYCOM

        Hà Nội

        Kỹ năng

        Sale Man

        Mô tả công việc

        - Setting goals and developing plans for business and revenue growth.
        - Researching, planning, and implementing new target market initiatives.
        - Researching prospective accounts in target markets.
        - Pursuing leads and moving them through the sales cycle.
        - Developing quotes and proposals for prospective clients.
        - Setting goals for the business development team and developing strategies to meet those goals.

        * Other responsibilities:
        - Training: Being responsible to train sales freshers/ Middle/ Sales Interns with different sales skills sessions assigned by the Sales Director.
        - Marketing: In a collaboration with Marketing Team to develop and mass out marketing campaigns.
        - Reporting: Making weekly/ monthly/ quarterly reports ( Savvycom is a data-driven organization).
        - Reviewing: Along with the Sales Director to review lead generation/ sales revenue/ sales closing to decide next action plans.

        Yêu cầu công việc

        The ideal candidate will demonstrate the following skill set:

        *Essential:
        - Smart, aggressive, ambitious to conquer new challenges (Hungry to earn money).
        - Excellent presentation & communication skills (ice-breaking, storytelling, and conversation maintenance).
        - Good negotiation, good listening & questioning skills, time management, and problem-solving skills.
        - Fluent in both spoken and written English (Advanced level) ( International Background is preferable).
        - Hard-working and self-accountable.
        - Communication skills: Must be able to communicate with clients of all backgrounds and educational levels. Well-developed written and verbal communication skills
        - Organizational skills: Must be comfortable juggling numerous clients, on- and off-site meetings, and deadlines, and to manage the many parts of the job and keep client proposals and other tasks on track.
        - Time-management skills: Must understand how to prioritize time and be comfortable managing a calendar and adjusting their schedule as needed.
        - Drive and self-motivation: Goal-oriented and commission-driven.
        - At least 05-year experience in Sales (preferably B2B) with a proven track record.
        - Year of Birth: After 1987
        *Desirable (Good to have):
        - Experience working/living in cross-culture, international environments.
        - Experience in the IT industry.

        Thời gian làm việc


        Quyền lợi ứng viên

        What You Will Get?
        By joining Savvycom, you will be a part of a B2B Enterprise Sales Alliance where you could find yourself in terms of becoming a better of yours.
        - Join the leadership and sales skills by training course by Savvycom
        - Become a Global Channel Sales approaching International C-Level Executives
        - Earn unlimited income from your sales achievement (upto 2500$ on base plus commision)
        - Have chances to stand in the Board Of Directors at Savvycom.
        - Other benefits following company policies (advance healthcare insurance, sports club, English club, leave days, holiday,…).
        - Salary review twice a year.
        - Be a part of a B2B Enterprise Sales Alliance where you could find yourself in terms of becoming a better of yours.
        - In addition to 12 days annual leave, leave of one day in the birthday month.
        - Receive social insurance according to Vietnamese Government regulations.
        - Working in a young and professional environment. Direct contact with English-speaking foreign clients.
        - Friendly & Open working environment (daily tea break, weekly sports time, monthly outdoor activity, annual holiday).
        - Support English courses, sport activities & other team building activities.
        - Working hours: 5 days/week (Monday to Friday).
