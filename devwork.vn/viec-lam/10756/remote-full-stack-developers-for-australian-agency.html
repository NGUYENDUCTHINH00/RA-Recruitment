        Remote Full-Stack Developers for Australian agency

        Marameo Design

        Đà Nẵng

        Kỹ năng

        PHPReactJSVueJSWordpress

        Mô tả công việc

        We are Marameo Design - a relaxed team based in Sydney and Vietnam. We offer a great work/life balance with a good salary, flexible hours and fast career advancement. 🙌 🤟 👌

        We are an agency that celebrates all types of diversity.

        We are looking for a team player that is excited to become part of our team and grow within the company.

        Attitude and fitting in our company culture are more important than skill-set.

        We are seeking a team player with excellent critical thinking and English skills.

        You need to be an autonomous doer, that gets stuff done and is happy to make decisions on your own.

        We are on a mission to help NFP organisations to fulfil their goals, and this role is pivotal to our and our client's success.

        You'll be planning, architecting and building high performance, accessible websites for some of the best organisations in Australia.

        This role would suit someone that has commercial experience as a web developer, ideally in multiple platforms, including WordPress.

        You'll be working with a team of other 3 developers in a remote environment and report to our Tech Lead.

        Yêu cầu công việc

        Not negotiable:

        🏆 Able to communicate with the client in meetings

        ️🏆 Good English skills (written and spoken)

        ️🏆 Able to effectively estimate tasks and projects

        🏆 Excellent testing, QA and self-management skills

        ️🏆 Critical thinking and autonomous

        ️🏆 Clean, safe code

        Responsibilities

        - Perform monthly support requests from clients (through the support ticket centre)
        - Develop custom WordPress themes for new client projects.
        - Develop and manage custom and complex WordPress-based solutions.
        - Create or customize plugin features to client websites (WordPress)
        - Ensuring high-performance and availability, and managing all technical aspects of the WordPress CMS.
        - Work to resolve issues related to WordPress websites and client hosting
        - Assist in maintaining client’s website speed performance and security.
        - Providing coaching and leadership to other members of the team on processes and best practices.
        - Helping to create a scalable development process.


        Experience

        You should have 3+ years of web development, both front and back end, specifically with WordPress and WooCommerce.
        The Developer will work with our project manager and lead developer to help design and implement high-performance, accessible products and experiences.

        You should have strong communication skills, also be confident enough to push back on the leadership team and express your opinion if you think the ship is heading off course.

        We encourage that.

        ✅ PHP and Object-Oriented languages

        ✅ HTML 5, CSS, SASS, JS / Library

        ✅ SSH Experience

        ✅ GIT (including branching), Version control and local environment setup

        ✅ Responsive Interface design

        ✅ Cross-browser compatibility issue resolution

        A bonus if you also have (all or any of those points):

        🔥 Experience with Drupal, Joomla or other CMS

        🔥 Headless CMS experience (Vue, Gridsome etc)

        🔥 Experience creating Gutemberg Blocks from scratch (ReactJS)

        🔥 Woo-Commerce experience

        Thời gian làm việc

        Trong tuần: Từ thứ 2 - thứ 6


        Quyền lợi ứng viên

        🚀 A remote/ freelancer work with an international team.

        💰 Great salary, bonuses and gifts (feel free to deal the salary based on your skills)

        🧘‍♂️ Work-life balance

        🍺 Professional, flexible and fun work environment.

        🏆 Complex and interesting projects from Small to Large Businesses.
