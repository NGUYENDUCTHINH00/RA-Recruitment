        DevOps Engineer (Hybrid)

        Spiderbox Design

        Hồ Chí Minh

        Kỹ năng

        DevOpsAzureAWS

        Mô tả công việc

        DevOps Engineer will be part of a fast growing team of developers to support a range of front end and back end projects for a large Australian client. You will be working with a large team of senior developers and QA initially to build velocity and support as a foundation team developing the front end to support a range of products. We work within an Agile SCRUM methodology.
        Domain: Energy
        Team size: 20 in VN and more than 100 in AU and NZ.
        - Report directly to team leader
        - Ability to work in an agile environment and iterate quickly based on customer and internal feedback.
        - Constantly learn and keep abreast of emerging technologies.
        - Accurate estimation and delivery of deadlines.

        Responsibilities
        - Create automation scripting/orchestration tool sets, e.g., Azure DevOps, GitHub.
        - Use git to version control scripts and programs.
        - Develop CI/CD pipelines using yaml/bicep, AzureCLI in Azure Devops
        - Refactor existing CI/CD pipelines and align with best practices
        - Performance tuning on app services and app service plans
        - Work with APIs (e.g., MS Graph API, SendGrid, Twilio) to develop CI/CD tools.
        - Use Jira and Confluence or similar products to build out a knowledge base and manage tickets.
        - Use of Postman, SOAP UI, XMLSpy, reading XML, JSON to use and troubleshoot APIs.
        - Follow and act on directions of senior staff or line managers.
        - Provide guidance to junior Devops resources.

        Yêu cầu công việc

        Qualifications and Education Requirements
        - At least 3 years of experience in the cloud space (preferably Azure)
        - Extensive experience with Azure services like Storage Account, APIM, Azure Front Door, Azure Functions, Azure Web Apps and Azure Service Bus
        - Extensive knowledge of YAML Pipelines
        - Networking knowledge within Azure
        - Experience with Docker and Managed Kubernetes Service
        - Experience with Infrastructure as Code and CI/CD pipelines
        - General IT skills, networking, server admin
        Prefer if having the following
        - Certification in Cloud DevOps (advantageous)
        - Experience working in an agile development environmentDevOps Engineer
        - Experience working with foreign companies, in particular, Australia
        - Interview process:
        + 1st round: English
        + 2nd round: Technical "

        Thời gian làm việc

        Trong tuần: Từ thứ 2 - thứ 6


        Quyền lợi ứng viên

        - 13th month salary
        - Monetary bonuses (Tet, holidays, birthday, marriage, etc)
        - Performance/salary review once per year: recognize your efforts and identify development/ improvement needs
        - Clear KPI’s and expectations
        - Free English class with foreign teachers weekly
        - Free lunch and snacks, motorbike parking at the office
        - Comfortable working time: Mon to Fri, 9am - 6pm (flexible) with 2 days remotely per week
        - 14 days off/ year, valid from probationary period
        - Private health care PTI (covering Covid-19), annual health check-up
        - Working devices with budget of 26M VND/ person
        - International, young, fun and respectful working environment
        - Internal activities: Company trip, Friday happy hours, English talks, Technical sharing,...

        Why you should apply for this position
        - Working in a convenient office in D2, HCMC
        - Comfortable working hours
        - Good salary and benefits package
        - Being a member of a fast growing company (double members in 2021 and continue to grow in 2022)
        - Tally project is huge and lots of things to learn and develop technical skill
        - Tally members are all young, fun, eager to learn, and super supportive
        - Tally leaders are super stars
