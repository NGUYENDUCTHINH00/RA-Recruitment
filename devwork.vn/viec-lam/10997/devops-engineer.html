        DevOps Engineer

        AdStart Media Pte Ltd

        Hồ Chí Minh

        Kỹ năng

        DevOpsAWSDocker

        Mô tả công việc

        We are offering an immediate opening for a DevOps Engineer based in our Ho Chi Minh office. The vacancy is ideal for an enthusiastic, energetic and versatile person ready to show his/her value in the fast and constantly changing digital advertising and mobile payments sectors.
        If you're looking for a dynamic experience that will push your potential, support your growth, give you meaningful work from the first day, and provide an environment where your voice can be heard in daily interactions with senior management, we are for you.

        **YOUR MAIN DUTIES:
        - Make an impact by building, monitoring and scaling a superb infrastructure during a period of rapid growth
        - Find solutions to complex infrastructure challenges with creative automation and configuration management solutions
        - Collaborate with related teams, build and support application environments that are deeply integrated with AWS
        - Develop custom tools and instrumentation to ensure high service availability
        - Implement and manage custom monitoring across the entire application stack, including 3rd party APIs
        - Perform root-cause analysis for production issues and recommend solutions
        - Setup, manage and monitor domains and SSL certificates

        Yêu cầu công việc

        - English fluency is a must
        - From 1-2 years of experience in Cloud platforms (AWS)
        - Experience in a Linux environment with bash scripting capabilities
        - Strong understanding of Amazon Web Services, such as: S3, EC2, ELB, API Gateway, Route53 etc.
        - Knowledge in automated build environments (GitLab CI, CodeCommit, CodePipeline)
        - Knowledge of databases like Cassandra, MySQL, PostgreSQL
        - Good understanding of networking, security, and basic protocols
        - Hand-on experience with containerization technologies (Docker) and orchestration tools (Kubernetes)
        - Practical experience with multiple Infrastructure as Code like CloudFormation for infrastructure provisioning in cloud environments (an ability to write and run modules, documents)
        - Experience working in agile development teams using Scrum methodology
        - Ability to work autonomously and within a team
        Nice to have
        - Previous server side development (ie: PHP, Python, Java, Scala, etc.)
        - AWS Certified Solution Architect - Associate

        Thời gian làm việc


        Quyền lợi ứng viên

        - English speaking multi-cultural environment
        - Flexible working time
        - Competitive salary and benefits
        - Premium medical insurance package (option to extend to family members)
        - Annual employee’s health-check
        - Generous annual leave days and paid sick leave days
        - Bi-Annual Performance Review
        - English classes / Toastmasters Clubs
        - Various trainings on soft-skills and best practices
        - Annual company trips, year end party and periodic team-building activities
