        Business Intelligence Engineer

        Công ty Cổ phần Công nghệ Neurond

        Hồ Chí Minh

        Kỹ năng

        AzureData Engineer

        Mô tả công việc

        - Collaborate to extend and manage Data Platform based on Azure stack and Power BI
        - Preparation of (PowerBI) BI reports with data produced in PowerPlatform or integrated from other systems (e.g ServiceNow, Salesforce, SAP, etc.)
        - Preparation of data storage systems (SharePoint, Dataverse etc.) in regard of solutions developed with Power Platform
        - Create, implement and manage master data maintenance solutions that enable users to optimize system capabilities
        - Design and implement efficient and maintainable data integration flows
        - Cleanse and enrich master data elements through end-to-end process analysis
        - Implement algorithms to improve data integrity and correctness and coordinate data management.

        Yêu cầu công việc

        Technical skills:
        - More than 5 years of experience in Data Modeling, Microsoft Azure components (data factory, databricks, etc.) and Microsoft SQL Server
        - Good knowledge of Design and development of new Information factories in the Microsoft Azure Cloud
        - Using Azure Cloud technology (Azure DevOps/ADF/Synapse/Tabular Models)
        - Create and maintain documentation that includes the KPI design, requirements and user manuals for the organization
        - Very good knowledge of Agile/Scrum and DevOps methodologies
        - Continuous integration and deployment skills
        - Experience with version control software and project management tools.

        Soft skills:
        - Be a problem solver with great problem solving skills
        - Proactive and effective self-learning skills and research
        - Good in English Reading and Writing skills
        - Effective verbal English skills in video conference and face to face communication.

        Thời gian làm việc


        Quyền lợi ứng viên

        - Attractive salary based on your performance
        - Annual 13th month salary
        - Dedication bonus goes up to 40% monthly salary
        - Project bonus will be paid twice a year
        - Premium healthcare insurance, Annual health checks up
        - Breakfast are available at office in early morning and snack in late afternoon
        - Team building allowance
        - Annual Company team building trip
        - Clubs (Football, Badminton, Swimming, Chess, Music band)
        - Exclusive training programs for all staff in technical, soft skills and process
        - English class
        - Technical clubs
        - Marriage allowance, having newborn baby allowance, bereavement allowance.

        5th floor, Suite 5.8, e.town 1 building, 364 Cong Hoa Str, Ward 13, Tan Binh Dist, Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam
