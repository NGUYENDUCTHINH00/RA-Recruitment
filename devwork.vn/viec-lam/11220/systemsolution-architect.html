        System/Solution Architect

        CÔNG TY TNHH CÔNG NGHỆ ASILLA VIỆT NAM

        Hà Nội

        Kỹ năng

        Software Architecture

        Mô tả công việc

        We're searching for a System/Solution Architect with experience and ability to design large-scale IT
        systems on On-premise or Cloud platforms. The System/Solution Architect will provide technology
        strategies to help our product achieve its business goals.
        Detail:
        ● Responsible for designing and implementing short and long-term strategic goals for managing
        and maintaining systems and software
        ● Design, create and monitor the implementation of end-to-end integrated systems
        ● Review new and existing system designs and make recommendations for improving or altering
        the systems
        ● Provide the expertise and architectural assistance to others in the development team
        ● Conduct research on new technologies in the field of systems development and create and
        develop plans for investing in such systems that will increase cost effectiveness and flexibility
        ● Consult with department heads and end-users to further infrastructure development
        ● Develop and execute test plans to check technical performance of infrastructure and report
        ● Identifying security risks and advising on how to mitigate them
        ● Determining hardware and software specifications for the system being designed

        Yêu cầu công việc

        Required skills and qualifications
        ● MS/BS degree in Computer Science, Engineering or equivalent preferred
        ● Over 4 years of experience working in System Architect position
        ● Proven experience developing strategic system architecture plans

        ● Experience in designing VMS (Video Management System), cloud systems
        ● Strong knowledge of software evaluation principles and practices
        ● Solid understanding of information processing fundamentals and best practices
        ● Excellent written and verbal communication skills
        ● Experience conducting technology, trends, standards and products research
        ● Solid track record in prioritizing and executing tasks when under extreme pressure
        ● Experience providing guidance and leadership to novice systems engineers
        ● Proven experience identifying, analyzing and resolving system problems
        ● Business English
        Preferred skills and qualifications
        ● Knowledge of AI domain
        ● Experience working in multinational companies

        Thời gian làm việc

        Trong tuần: Từ thứ 2 - thứ 6


        Quyền lợi ứng viên

        ● Competitive salary: up to $3500
        ● 14 months' salary a year
        ● Opportunity to receive Stock Option
        ● To fully participate in the regimes prescribed by the State such as social insurance, health
        insurance, unemployment insurance, annual leave
        ● Participate in team building sessions, company travel, have fun monthly with the Company
        ● Enjoy full benefits (Happiness, birthday...)
        ● Young, comfortable working environment, dynamic startup spirit

        Địa chỉ làm việc

        No. 6, lane 82 Duy Tan-Cau Giay-HN
