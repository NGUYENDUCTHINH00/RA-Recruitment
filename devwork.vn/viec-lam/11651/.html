        .NET Developer (remote)

        Công ty Cổ phần Phần mềm Devwork

        Tất cả địa điểm

        Kỹ năng

        .NET

        Mô tả công việc

        Join our Singaporan client's project
        Qualification process:
        Pre-qualification process with a short interview (15-20 mins) will be conducted.
        3 rounds of interviews with client’s CTO and their team.

        Yêu cầu công việc

        Requirements (Experience & Skills)
        At least 4 years of experience as a developer in financial services industry
        Full software development lifecycle experiences with good design and programming principles. Able to design smart, reusable, scalable, efficient code following the organization’s standards.
        Experience in analysis, design, develop, test, document, and support of medium-large scale applications.
        Ability to translate business requirements into technical design documents.
        Experience in OOP, with intermediate to advance skill in Python and .NET programming, common libraries, and framework.
        Basic knowledge of system architecture, network protocol and standards, security as well as cloud technologies.
        Strong RDBMS skills and writing effective SQL, stored procedures, and jobs.
        Experience in creating data integration pipelines to extract, transform and integrate from a variety of sources and formats.
        Possess excellent debugging and performance fine tuning skills.
        Good knowledge in Agile Development (TDD, Scrum, Kanban, CI/CD etc)
        Familiarity and confidence working in an agile development environment.
        Experience with source code version control systems.
        Familiarity with MS Azure/AWS, .Net Framework/Core, Entity Framework, Angular and ReactJS.
        Experience with supporting and updating a live system with multiple clients.
        Experience with AWS Cloud/DevOps is a plus.
        Knowledge of financial products, markets, instruments, and trade processing functions is a high plus.
        Interpersonal Skills
        Passion and flair for technology
        Good interpersonal skills to deal with highly complex and challenging business facing environments and technology teams.
        Attention to code quality.
        Excellent problem-solving abilities and judgement with strong team spirit.
        Desire to succeed in a challenging environment and work independently and effectively.
        Able to articulate clearly, demonstrate strong verbal and written communication skills.
        Self-started, self-driven, problem solver with strong ownership and commitment.
        Education
        Recognised university degree in Computer Science, Engineering or related field.
        Proficient in English, both written and spoken

        Thời gian làm việc

        Trong tuần: Từ thứ 2 - thứ 6

        Trong ngày: Từ 08:30 giờ - 18:00 giờ


        Quyền lợi ứng viên

        - No need to try a job, get an official job with 100% salary
        - Opportunities onsite and working with big customers, advanced technology, self-development...
        - The opportunity to work in groups with many leading experts in the field of domestic and international IT.
        - Opportunity to implement ambitious projects in many countries, exposure to the latest technologies and learn from good colleagues.
        - Working in a young, vibrant, modern and multicultural environment; Communication activities and events on holidays take place regularly.
        - Opportunity for advancement based on ability with corresponding increase in rank and salary increase.
        - Have the right to participate in soft skills training courses (logical thinking, creative thinking, communication skills, project management skills, negotiation skills…) and Japanese language classes.
        - And many other attractive benefits...

        [QST] Remote 100%
