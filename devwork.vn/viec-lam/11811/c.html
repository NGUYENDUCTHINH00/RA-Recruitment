        Mô tả công việc

        Key Responsibilities and Duties:
        • Able to develop local systems or EUC tools
        • BAU support for system issues in HNI/HCM
        • Back up for other team members in promoting projects
        • Enhance quality to avoid re-work later
        • Others works assigned by Department Head

        Yêu cầu công việc

        Requirements:
        Education Level/certificates:
        - Essential: University graduated in software engineering
        - Preferable:
        + Certificate on software development (if possible, C#.NET)
        + DBA certificate on Oracle, MSSQL
        + Able to communicate in English
        Experience and skills:
        - Essential:
        • More than 3-5 years of software development experience as Developer and preferable if having PM or BA roles where projects used C# .NET, SQL Server, Javascript, CSS etc.
        • Good sense of teamwork and responsibility, able to work under high pressure with accuracy.
        • Excellent troubleshooting and communication skills with strong attention to detail.
        - Preferable:
        • Working experience with big projects in foreign company is a plus;
        • Experience in MSSQL, Oracle DBA role or Certificate of Software development (E.g. C#.NET) is preferable
        • Experience in Business Analysis or Project Management or technical team lead with framework building.
        • Experienced to build framework and in big project.
        - Required Skill:
        • Customers oriented mindset and logical thinking.
        • Have a good communication skill, time management skill as well as self-management and reporting skills.
        • Be able to work independently but uphold teamwork spirit as well.
        • Have a good working attitude and good characteristics.

        Thời gian làm việc

        Trong tuần: Từ thứ 2 - thứ 6

        Trong ngày: Từ 08:30 giờ - 18:00 giờ


        Quyền lợi ứng viên

        - No probationary period, receive 100% official salary, Onsite allowances 100.000 VND/day
        - Opportunities onsite and working with big customers, advanced technology, self-development...
        - The opportunity to work in groups with many leading experts in the field of domestic and international IT.
        - Opportunity to implement ambitious projects in many countries, exposure to the latest technologies and learn from good colleagues.
        - Working in a young, vibrant, modern and multicultural environment; Communication activities and events on holidays take place regularly.
        - Opportunity for advancement based on ability with corresponding increase in rank and salary increase.
        - Have the right to participate in soft skills training courses (logical thinking, creative thinking, communication skills, project management skills, negotiation skills…)
        - And many other attractive benefits...
