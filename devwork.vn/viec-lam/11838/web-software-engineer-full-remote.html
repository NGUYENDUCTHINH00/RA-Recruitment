        Web Software Engineer Full Remote

        Nexus Frontier Tech

        Hà Nội

        Kỹ năng

        GolangPHPPythonVueJSReact Native

        Mô tả công việc

        - Engage with clients, partners, and other stakeholders to determine the objectives of the project. You will identify and analyze the technical challenges that need to be addressed to meet these objectives, and then take the lead in resolving them.
        - Create technical specifications and design the architectural framework based on the project's goals, and future vision, and in line with current software development methodologies and technologies.
        - Take charge of translating the architectural design and specifications into actionable development tasks. You will be responsible for all aspects of software development, skillfully handling tasks across various domains, including front-end, back-end, and DevOps.
        - Collaborate closely with the Quality Consultant (QC) and Project Manager to ensure software quality, playing an active role in quality assurance processes.
        - Along with your primary project duties, you will participate in multiple projects and take on the role of a mentor and consultant for Tech Members, guiding and supporting them as needed.
        - Perform additional challenging tasks as assigned by the BOM/Director, with details to be further discussed during the interview.

        Yêu cầu công việc

        - Minimum 5 years of combined experience in both frontend and backend web development, including:
        - Backend development using languages such as PHP, Golang, Python, and Rust.
        - Frontend development utilizing modern frameworks such as React and Vue.js.
        - Working with Relational databases (e.g., MySQL, PostgreSQL) and key-value pair (NoSQL) databases, including schema design and scalability management.
        - Comprehensive design of system architecture for web services and mobile apps.
        - Web API interface creation ( Such as REST/GraphQL ) for web frontend and mobile interfaces.
        - System infrastructure planning for cloud services (AWS, GCP, Azure), and on-premises systems, with considerations for the operation work such as monitoring and data backup.
        - Experience in testing, including writing unit test code, designing test systems, and automating tests.
        - More than 2 years of experience as a technical lead, with responsibilities for managing and training junior team members.
        - Proven experience working in an international remote team, with proficiency in both written and verbal English communication.
        - Goal-Oriented Mindset: The ability to see beyond the task assigned and take a holistic view of the entire product building and software development process. You should be proactive in addressing any gaps, even if they fall outside your specific responsibilities.
        - Fullstack Mindset: This goes beyond web development to encompass a willingness to work proactively on anything related to system and product development. This includes but is not limited to, web backend and frontend, mobile development, infrastructure construction, and AI model development.
        - Eagerness to Learn Cutting-Edge Technology: You should be passionate about staying abreast of the latest technologies and be willing to share this knowledge with team members.
        - Excellent Communication Skills: Effective international communication is key. You should understand and appreciate cultural differences, and be able to communicate effectively with people from various backgrounds and mindsets.
        - Good conversational English

        Thời gian làm việc

        Trong tuần: Từ thứ 2 - thứ 6


        Quyền lợi ứng viên

        Offer of up to $2,000+ salary, based on experience and capacity (negotiable).
        13-month salary and monthly financial support policies, monthly club activities.
        Salary review - twice per year.
        Extra Private health insurance coverage by the company to help you get better focus on working.
        Flexible working time schedule and leave policy as we know the importance of work-life balance.
        - Be exposed to the latest technology and the most innovative ideas worldwide.
        - Yearly Medical checkup.
        - Budget for monthly group activities, such as outdoor activities, etc. 10 USD/per person/month after probation time.
        - Budget for ones who have children up to 18 years old (1st child: 50 USD/ month, 2nd child & onwards 25 USD/month).
        - Self-host events: up to 100 USD/ event (flexible support amount is up to the event’s topic that is based on the scale and facts).
        - Other benefits: hospitalization (>=4 days), wedding, funeral, and offspring delivery support: 1,000,000 VND once.
        - Yearly events: team building activities, Year-end party, o, etc.
